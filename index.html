Our Vision and Roadmap

Till We Hit 10k

For 10k followers, Pillow plans to select a location to build the company for 10 days and sponsor a member from the community to join them.

Till We Hit 100k

At 100k followers, Pillow aims to select a group of people from the community and send them on a fully sponsored trip.

Till We Hit 500k

Upon reaching 500k followers, Pillow plans to host its own physical creators networking event and reveal a product for creators.

Our Journey

We started in 2015 when content creation wasn’t a boom. Ritik was a marketer, and Manthan was a singer-songwriter. With our team, we created songs and streamed interactively for five years, but it didn’t work out as planned. We then pivoted to our individual pursuits; Manthan opened his label and audio production house, and Ritik launched his software company in Dubai.

After 3-4 years, we realized our passion for content creation and decided to unite our efforts. Here we are, introducing Pillow - a community-driven platform.
